STEP 2: Get Certificate for App Signing (5 minutes)
----------------------------------------------------
Run this command in your terminal:

   eas credentials -p android

Then:
1. Select: production
2. Select: credentials.json
3. Select: Download credentials from EAS to credentials.json
4. Select the NEWEST keystore

This downloads the keystore to: credentials/android/keystore.jks

Then extract certificate (copy the password and alias from credentials.json):

   keytool -export -rfc -keystore credentials\android\keystore.jks 
   -storepass [PASSWORD] -alias [ALIAS] 
   -file pulsemate-patient-certificate.pem

STEP 3: Setup App Signing in Play Console (2 minutes)
------------------------------------------------------

2. Click "Use App Signing by Google Play"
